## Long Description

Workbench gives your Mac a faster place to work with files.

It combines a Finder-style browser with power tools for media-heavy folders, notes, snippets,
cleanup, and disk analysis. It is built for creators, developers, students, and anyone who regularly
sorts screenshots, videos, downloads, PDFs, recordings, and project folders.

### Highlights

- Browse folders in list, icon, and column-style workflows.
- Preview images, videos, and audio without opening another app.
- Resize thumbnails and preview media directly inside the workspace.
- Rotate, flip, convert, and merge supported media files.
- Take notes with pasted or dragged images, audio, video, and file attachments.
- Save reusable snippets of text, images, audio, and files with quick copy/open actions.
- Record voice notes and play them back inside the app.
- Analyze disk usage with charts and top deletion candidates.
- Filter files quickly by date, size, type, file, or folder.
- Right-click common files for focused image, video, audio, PDF, document, archive, and folder tools.
- Move selected files into a new folder, create text files quickly, and keep the result selected.

### Built For

- Photo and video sorting

- Student research and journaling
- Project folder organization
- Finding large old files before moving them to an external drive

## FAQ

### Is this a Finder replacement?

No. Workbench is a focused workspace for heavier file tasks. Finder stays on your Mac; Workbench
adds faster browsing, media tools, notes, snippets, and cleanup workflows.

### Does Workbench upload my files?

No. Workbench works locally on your Mac. It does not include analytics, cloud sync, or background
uploads.

### Why does macOS ask for permissions?

macOS controls access to screen recording and the microphone. Workbench only asks when those tools
need access.

### Can I use it on more than one Mac?

Yes. One Workbench purchase covers you and members of your household on up to 5 Macs. A household
license is for personal/family use, not company-wide sharing or resale.

### How do updates work?

Customers can download updated builds from their Gumroad library when new versions are published.
