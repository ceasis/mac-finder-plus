# Workbench Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-12

Workbench is a local macOS utility. It is designed to work with files on your Mac.

## Data Collection

Workbench does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, account tracking, or cloud sync.

## Files

Workbench reads and modifies files only when you choose folders, select files, or run file tools.
Your files stay on your Mac unless you move, copy, share, or export them yourself.

## Local App Data

Workbench may store local preferences, notes, snippets, bookmarks, thumbnails, and disk analysis
results in your macOS user account so the app can restore your workspace.

## Permissions

Workbench may request:

- Screen Recording permission for screenshot and screen recording tools.
- Microphone permission for voice recording, voice notes, and narrated recordings.

These permissions are controlled by macOS and can be changed in System Settings.
